Voting

Why vote?

Voting for Project Eden helps raise our ranking on the server lists. A higher ranking means more players will see us as they browse for a server to join. More players mean more potential staff members, donors, and normal players, making Project Eden a better place.

Voting is the quickest and easiest way of showing your support for Project Eden and ensuring it will last. And don't forget about the nice rewards :)

Vote Rewards

Vote Parties

As more people vote, the count towards a new Vote Party increases. When the Vote Partyâ€™s goal is met, every player who voted during the period will receive an away. These can trigger at any time and have no specified end date. Rewards include things like crate keys, personal boosts, and helpful survival items.

Monthly Top Voting Rewards

At the end of each month, the people with the most votes receive the following rewards:

First $10 store credit
Second $5 store credit
Third $3 store credit

Vote Points Store

Each time you vote, you receive one vote point (and a small chance of getting more) to spend in the /vps. This in-game store has many different helpful items across many different game modes.

Daily Vote Streak

Every day you vote on at least two out of the four vote sites, your Vote Streak will increase.

Streak Reward
3 Days 5 Extra Vote Points
5 Days 5 Vote Crate Keys
10 Days 10 Vote Crate Keys
15 Days 20 Vote Crate Keys
30 Days 1 Random Personal Boost

The rewards will be automatically mailed to you in-game. Additionally, the rewards loop every 30 days. This means that on day 40, you will receive the same reward as day 10, and on day 90, you will get the same reward as day 30, etc.

* Everything is subject to change at the discretion of the staff.
